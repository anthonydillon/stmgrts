ALE CLUB

We are simultaneously launching our new realale.com Ale Club, within which we offer two different membership levels, the 'Classic' Membership with monthly delivery, and 'Session' Membership with bi-monthly delivery.

  1. 'Classic' Membership
    You will receive one case of 12 ales each calendar month, containing 4 different ales, 3 bottles of each brew. Each of the ales selected in the case are hand picked by Nick for their quality, uniqueness and taste. Also included in the case will be a full listing of his tasting notes for the individual ales, information about each of the brewers and, on occasion, a few other freebies. The cost of this 'Classic' Membership case is only £27.10/month — along with which you will also receive a blanket 10% discount for the life of your 'Classic' Membership for any other purchases made online at realale.com.
  2. 'Session' Membership
    You will receive one case of 12 ales every second calendar month, containing 3 different ales, 4 bottles of each brew. Each of the ales in this case are hand picked by Nick for their ease of drinking. Also included in the case will be a full listing of his tasting notes for the individual ales and information about each of the brewers. The cost of this club case is £28.75/month — along with which you will also receive a blanket 10% discount for the life of your 'Session' Membership for any other purchases made online at realale.com.
