What are you links to the North Twickenham/St Margarets community?

Lived in Twickenham since 1998. Chair of Cole Park Residents Association for 5 years. Chair Kew Wind Orchestra for 10 years. Member of RFU liaison committees. Currently work in Sunbury.

Why did/do you want to be a councilor?

To improve the area in which I live for everyone here with particular regard for the needs of older people as well as maker the area generally safer and more environmentally aware.

Issues

What do you think are the top three issues for North Twickenham/St Margarets and what do you should be done about them?

  1. Improve Twickenham town centre and St Margarets in terms of retail environment, traffic and parking.
  2. Promote sports and the arts more across the borough.
  3. Promote carbon neutrality in terms of recycling, energy efficiency and creating green space and planting more trees..

What areas are you passionate about and what committees would you like to sit on?

Passionate about music and the arts. Want to be actively involved in sports and arts related decisions as well as town planning and environment development.

Are there any big things you are looking to achieve or change?

Improve Twickenham town centre. Encourage development of performing arts venues and societies.

If elected, how will you keep in-touch with your constituents?

Be active with residents associations. Ward surgeries. Use of e-mail and website.
