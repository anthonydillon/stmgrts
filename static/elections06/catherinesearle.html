Issues

What do you think are the top three issues for North Twickenham/St Margarets and what do you should be done about them?

  1. Council Tax – continuing to keep levels down, and communication to residents about what it is spent on and what difference it makes
  2. Aircraft Noise – continuing to campaign for a cap on volume of flights and no night flights
  3. Youth Issues – more funding for voluntary sector youth activities and for local youth amenities

What areas are you passionate about and what committees would you like to sit on?

I am passionate about high standards of social care, especially for children & young people, people with disabilities, and older people. I would like to sit on the Overview and Scrutiny Committee for Social Care and Housing.

Are there any big things you are looking to achieve or change?

I want to build on the sense of community St Margarets already enjoys, and ensure Council support and funding for positive community-building initiatives from St Margarets & North Twickenham residents.

If elected, how will you keep in-touch with your constituents?

Email & telephone, occasional surgeries and regular newsletters.
