Background

Tell us about your education?

GCSE O level standard went on to Technical College. Qualified as an Electronics and TV Technician.

What are three jobs you have held?

  1. Senior Technician with a national electrical firm.
  2. Manager/ Proprietor of small businesses.

What are you links to the North Twickenham/St Margarets community?

I moved to North Twickenham in 1985 when I bought and ran my first business. I have lived here ever since, running other small businesses whilst playing an active role in the community and the Save the Children Charity Fundraising. Both my children were educated by local schools and Richmond Upon Thames College, and have gone on to further education.

Why did/do you want to be a councilor?

I believe I am willing and able to make a beneficial impact on the community. I also hope to make as many friends as possible.

Issues

What do you think are the top three issues for North Twickenham/St Margarets and what do you should be done about them?

  1. Parking Problems in and around Moor Meads need resolving, no easy answer but need to make resources immediately available.
  2. Plastics and Cardboard recycling needs to be introduced ASAP
  3. Wards should be subdivided and key local decisions should be taken in consultation with the representatives of these subdivisions perhaps Chairs of Residents Associations

What areas are you passionate about and what committees would you like to sit on?

Old and vulnerable people care.

Are there any big things you are looking to achieve or change?

I want to ensure that the residents concerns are effectively represented at the Council. I also wish to create a feeling of community harmony.

If elected, how will you keep in-touch with your constituents?

Vince Cable-like surgeries, email, website?

All of the above. I think I would like to “copy” Vince in all that he does. Of course I would continue to keep in touch with the Lib Dem “comments”
