Additionally a new group has formed, the Moormead Park Group, made up of local residents and representatives of the cricketers, footballers, St MargaretsÕ Fair Committee and local businesses. £15000 profit for this year’s St Margarets Moormead July Fair

Record attendance this year brought record profits. As always the organisers ensure that the profit is re-distributed to local charities and schools. Only a small amount is kept back to pay for the pre-fair expenses of the following yearÕs Fair. The schools receive money on a points system based on their input to the organisation of the Fair and on their Ôon the dayÕ support. Selected charities are approached at the start of each year and invited to give a ten minute presentation about their work. The Fair Committee then decide who the supported charities will be.
