----------------------------------------------------------------------

TALK: SUFFRAGETTES, SUFFRAGISTS AND ANTIS - 26 MARCH 2019
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Suffragettes, Suffragists and Antis is the subject of Hounslow and
    District History Society's next meeting on Tuesday 26 March 2019
    at the United Reformed Church Hall, Chapel Road, Hounslow, TW3 1UL
    commencing at 7.45pm. The lecturer is Mrs Irene Cockcroft,
    authoress and lecturer specialising in women's involvement in the
    Arts and Crafts and Suffragette movements.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/03/talk-suffragettes.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



MAJOR GAS WORKS ON RICHMOND ROAD
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Residents, businesses and commuters are being notified of major gas
    mains upgrade works taking place Richmond Road, which are planned
    to begin on 25th March and will continue for approximately
    eight weeks.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/03/lbrut-richmond-road-gas-works.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



COUNCIL AWARDED NEW FUNDING TO TACKLE ROUGH SLEEPING
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Richmond Council has been awarded &pound;223,000 from a pot of cash
    the Government has set aside as part of a Rapid Rehousing Pathway
    initiative. The scheme aims to significantly reduce rough sleeping
    across the country.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/03/lbrut-awarded-funds-for-rough-sleepers.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



CURATOR'S TALK AT TURNER'S HOUSE - 6 APRIL 2019
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Join historian and curator Dr Jacqueline Riding as she discusses
    J.M.W. Turner's exquisite book illustrations for two of Sir Walter
    Scott's epic Poetical Works and The Life of Napoleon Buonaparte.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/03/event-curators-talk-turner-house.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
