EVENT REMINDER : A COMMUNITY PLAN FOR ST MARGARETS -- 6TH JUNE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    An initial meeting has been arranged for Tuesday 6th June, 7.30pm 
    at Winchester Hall, Winchester Road (by the Turks Head pub and St
    Stephens Jnr. School). Representatives from various community 
    groups, the police, our new councillors as well as individual
    residents will be attending, but we will need the views and
    support of as many people as possible if the project is to develop
    into a practical community plan.
