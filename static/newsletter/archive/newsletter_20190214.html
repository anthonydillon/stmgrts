An amended 20mph limit is set to be introduced in Richmond upon Thames, if the Council's Scrutiny Committee, followed by the Cabinet, agree to the amended proposals. This follows a 12-week consultation held last year.

Step-free entrance and ticket gates


A new step-free access gate will be installed at St Margarets station to make travelling easier for people with mobility issues. The scheme is being jointly funded by South Western Railway and the London Borough of Richmond.
