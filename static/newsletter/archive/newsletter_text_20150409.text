UNANIMOUS APPROVAL FOR TURNER'S HOUSE RESTORATION PLANS
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    The London Borough of Richmond upon Thames Planning Committee has
    unanimously approved the restoration and conservation plans
    for Sandycombe Lodge, Twickenham, the country villa JMW Turner
    designed as a country retreat for himself and his father.

    Turner's House Trust will now be able to progress with its plans
    to conserve for the nation this unique Grade II* listed building,
    which is currently on English Heritage's 'Heritage at
    Risk' register.
