----------------------------------------------------------------------
    
    FORTEAN (adj.)
    'Pertaining to extraordinary phenomenon and happenings'

    If you have recently walked up the path by the Thames, from
    Richmond Bridge to Hammerton's Ferry, you may have noticed an
    elephant lurking in the bushes. Whether it is an actual elephant or
    something that just looks like an elephant is neither here nor
    there but it would have been of considerable interest to American
    writer and researcher Charles Hoy Fort. He would even have had a
    name for such a curiosity - Simulacra.
