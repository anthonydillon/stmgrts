MEETING NOTES : ST MARGARETS COMMUNITY PLAN
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    About sixty people attended the meeting at Winchester Hall, 
    to discuss the possibility of a local community plan for 
    St Margarets. It is anticipated that the community plan will be 
    a based on community values and provide the borough bureaucracies,
    local councillors, other groups and residents with the guidelines
    to maintain and improve the quality of life in St Margarets.
    Also attending the meeting were the three new local councillors,
    elected on 4th May, four community police officers, and
    a representative from the council's planning department.


    o  read more on the website 
       http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/archives/2006/06/meeting_notes_s.html
