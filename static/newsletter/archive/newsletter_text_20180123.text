A GREAT UNCLE RETURNS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    
    Sarah Foot who lives in St Margarets is one of three sisters;
    Judith, Lucy and herself. Just before last Christmas she received a
    phone call from Lucy. Instead of the usual family chitchat
    about this and that Lucy told her that she had received an
    unexpected phone call from the Ministry of Defence.

FIVE WEEK MINDFULNESS AND EMOTION COURSE - WINTER 2018
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    'Tending the flame of the heart' will take place on Wednesday
    evenings from February 14th - March 21st, (excluding March 14th)
    from 7:30-9:30pm. We will meet for four consecutive weeks and then
    have our final class after a two week break. The venue for the
    course is a family home on Laurel Avenue in Twickenham and the cost
    is 95 pounds.
