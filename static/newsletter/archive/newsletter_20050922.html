Latest News


Application for Wine Bar Re-submitted

A group of local businessmen (Grapespot Ltd of Kingston) have resubmitted their application to open a wine bar in the basement formerly occupied by Phelps. This application requires a change of use (A4) to the property. Grapespot plan to call the wine bar ParVinu at Phelps and offer retail wine sales, wine tastings and seating and a bar for drinking and dining on site.

This story has attracted a lot of community interest and the poll shows that people strongly dislike the idea (more than 80% don’t want it). Most are concerned with the size of the venue, what happens if it doesn’t attract an upscale crowd — but a young rowdy one or if the wine bar ever fails, what is someone worse moves as the planning permission has been alredy changed.

This St Margaret Community Website is going to interview the men behind Grapespot next week, let us know your questions (content@stmgrts.org.uk).
