St Margarets Autumn Event Needs Your Help

The St Margarets Autumn Event will be held in Crown Road on Saturday, 17 September 2005. The event is organised by a combined group of residents and traders and sponsored by the RFU. It promises to be a great community event with live music, food stalls, fun fair rides and more.

However, they need more groups in the Community to get involved with the event!

One of the main goals of the event is for local people to raise funds for local projects. Fair organisers are still looking for local groups to come forward and set-up a stall or run a game at the fair.

The fair organisers, the St Margarets Trader’s Association (traders@stmgrts.org.uk, need to hear from you if you would like to help with fund raising or help with organising the event.

Third Annual North St Margarets Fun Day

All of St Margarets is invited to attend the Third Annual North St Margarets Fun Day on Saturday, 10 September.

A wide variety of events have been planned, including:

  • from 11 - 12, a Children’s fancy dress walk called ‘Beating the Bounds’
  • from 2 - 4:30, a Street Sale where you can buy and sell your household and garden extras

Interview : Nick Dolan of Real Ale

In May of this year, Nick Dolan opened a new local business, Real Ale on the high street in East Twickenham. As you might have guessed from the name, Real Ale offers a wide selection of the finest real ales and ciders that are produced by British micro-breweries. Previously, you could only get these by visiting the breweries or a few local outlets.
