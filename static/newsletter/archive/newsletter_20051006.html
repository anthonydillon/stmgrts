Familiar Friend Has Passed Away

Local character Dylan, the chocolate brown Labrador owned by the Herdman family, has passed away this past 4th of October. He had fought cancer over the last four years, but finally lost his fight.

Late License Decision for the Turks Head

The Licensing Committee has met and given the Turks Head substantially reduced hours from what they had been asking.

The hours they have been given are as follows:

Pub

Sunday - Thursday 10am - 11.30pm
Friday & Saturday 10am - midnight

Winchester Hall

Sunday - Thursday 10am - 11.30pm
Friday & Saturday 10am - 1am

30 minutes drinking up time for both.
