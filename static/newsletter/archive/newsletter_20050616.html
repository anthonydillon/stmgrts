Latest News

Tesco, Stop Pushing Around St Margarets

A local group called SPASM (for Stop Pushing Around St Margarets ) has been actively campaigning to make local Tesco conform with existing regulations and making sure the Richmond Council doesn’t pander to the retail giant’s whims unfairly.

The Twichenham Challenge

The regeneration of the Twickenham Riverside is well under way with Stage 1 offically completed on 1 June with the opening of the Jubilee Gardens, a playground and a soon to be opened CafÃ©.

The second Stage is titled “The Twickenham Challenge.” The Council is working with local and national groups to develop proposals for self-sustaining facilities, representing 15-20% of the total space of the site. The goal is for the Council to review and approve plans by this summer.

— read more

St Margarets Blossoms

After several months of planning and negotiations hanging baskets have finally arrived in St. Margarets Road and Crown Road. The baskets have been organised by the St. Margarets Traders’ Association working in partnership with Richmond in Bloom. Funding for the baskets was helped through the Richmond Council’s excellent Civic Pride scheme.

— read more

St Margarets Community Event September 2005

A partnership of local traders, residents and school pta’s are currently planning a community event to be held in Crown Road in September, the exact date is still to be confirmed. The event will be aimed at raising funds for local projects and charities.
