Residents try to reclaim Rosslyn Road from protesters

The “Reclaim Rosslyn Road” petition concerning the disruption caused by protesters outside the BPAS abortion clinic in Rosslyn Road has now closed with 390 signatures, almost all of which came from roads in the immediate vicinity of the clinic.

Local Artist Sets off in the footsteps of Turner

I will be setting off around the east and south coasts of England in Spring 2015 to paint at the locations chosen by Turner for his Ports and Harbours of England series. This will take me from Yorkshire to Cornwall in 15 steps, stopping at the some of the most famous towns in English maritime history.
