AMERICAN PIE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    
    "This is a story about America."
     - DON McLEAN

    42 years ago, on the 22nd January 1972, a record by American singer
    Don McLean reached No. 2 in the UK charts. "American Pie" was and
    remains a significant song of the period and has been voted No. 5
    in the 100 most important recordings of the 20th century. For an
    entire generation it marked the end of the 1960s and put a final
    full stop on their teenage years. In a series of cryptic verses the
    song tells how the popular music they had grown up with had lost
    its innocence.
