MARBLE HILL REVIVED
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    royal court and the owner of a handsome Palladian mansion set in 72
    acres of prime Thames riverside. That lay ahead.

BOROUGH WIDE 20MPH LIMIT MOVES FORWARD
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    An amended 20mph limit is set to be introduced in Richmond
    upon Thames, if the Council's Scrutiny Committee, followed by the
    Cabinet, agree to the amended proposals. This follows a 12-week
    consultation held last year.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/02/lbrut-20mph-limit-moves-to-committee.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter

STEP-FREE ENTRANCE AND TICKET GATES
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    A new step-free access gate will be installed at St Margarets
    station to make travelling easier for people with mobility issues.
    The scheme is being jointly funded by South Western Railway and the
    London Borough of Richmond.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/02/step-free-entrance-for-station.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



FREE INFORMATION EVENING FOR EU CITIZENS IN MARCH
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    A free public information evening for EU citizens living in the
    borough will be held at Richmond and Hillcroft Adult Community
    College, Parkshot TW9 2RE from 6pm on 5th March 2019.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/02/event-lbrut-eu-citizens.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter



PLANT SALE FOR WILDLIFE AT KILMOREY - 28 APRIL 2019
----------------------------------------------------------------------

    Sunday 28th April 2019, 10am - 3pm at Kilmorey Mausoleum
    wildlife garden.

    After the huge success of last year's Plant Sale, Environment Trust
    is running its second Plant Sale and combining it with an Open Day
    at the Grade II listed Kilmorey Mausoleum in St Margarets on the
    28th April 2019.

    o  read more on the website
       https://stmargarets.london/archives/2019/02/event-kilmorey-open-day-plant-sale.html?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=traffic%2Bfrom%20text%20newsletter
