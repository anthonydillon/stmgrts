AGE UK RICHMOND DOORSTEP SAFETY PACKS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    
    Age UK Richmond upon Thames are working in partnership with the
    Safer Neighbourhood Team and Trading Standards to help keep you
    safer from doorstep crime. We want to give you the confidence to
    say 'no' to cold calling traders on your doorstep. We've put
    together a Doorstep Safety Pack which contains anti-cold caller
    door stickers to deter rogue callers and to remind you if unsure,
    don't open the door.

    We are also offering free supply and fitting of a range of doorstep
    security devices.
