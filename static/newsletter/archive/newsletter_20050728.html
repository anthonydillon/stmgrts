St Margarets Autumn Fun Day

The Council have now given permission for the road closure on Saturday 17th September. This means that the St Margarets Autumn Fun day can now go ahead. We would like to celebrate what a great place this is to live and to represent all the sporting and community activities available in the area.

To get the ball rolling the St Margarets Tavern have kindly agreed to sponsor the stage and a band. Crown Road will be closed to traffic from the St Margarets Tavern to Hartington Road between 10am and 4pm.

There will be lots of space to fill and this is your chance to get involved. If you have an idea for a stall, If you have an idea for entertainment let us know on the website. No one person is behind this it is a mixture of the traders and some of the residents so this is your chance to get involved. Email (simonchapman@stm.demon.co.uk).or call into Simon in Cutters 28 Crown Road with your ideas.

If you would like to raise funds for your group this is the place to do it. There is no charge to these groups if the money they make is being recycled into the community. If you are a trader and would like to purchase a stall in order to sell your own products you would pay a small fee. This money will go into a general collection and be used towards the cost of next years hanging baskets. The more money raised the more we can do together.
