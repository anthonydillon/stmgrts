PLANNING APPLICATION: FORMER CROWN ROAD SURGERY
----------------------------------------------------------------------
    The owners or the building that used to be the Crown Road Surgery,
    at 66 Crown Road have submitted a planning application to turn 
    the property into four flats, including:
    
     - two 2 bedroom flats
     - one 1 bedroom flat
     - one studio flat
     
     
    They also seek to retain the two doctor’s parking spaces as private
    spaces for the residents.
    
    o  read the application
       http://idoxwam.richmond.gov.uk/WAM/findCaseFile.do;jsessionid=23F0D248A60A68E52616DA7D81D58DFD?appNumber=07%2F4239%2FFUL&action=Search
