Dreamcatcher summer filmmaking camp - July 2017

It's that time of year again as we look towards summer and the film camp. As always the setting is the Falcons Prep. The films made on our Summer Film Camps feature great heroes and anti-heroes, goodies and baddies, exciting chases and dramatic scenes - all scripted, filmed and directed by young people aged 8 - 18.

Balancing workshop for teens - 7 June 2017

Announcing a new balancing yoga workshop for 14 to 18 year olds. A 6 week course starting at the ETNA Centre in East Twickenham, starting on Wed 7th of June.

Come to your free trial class and explore the joys of being upside down and arm balances. Find that subtle connection between your body and mind through synching your movement with your breath, build your core strength and confidence, improve your concentration and learn how to relax your mind.
