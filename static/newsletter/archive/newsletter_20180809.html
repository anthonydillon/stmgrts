Find your street's cleaning schedule


A new online interactive tool has been launched to help local people find out when their streets should be cleaned. The borough's street cleansing contract is outsourced to an external provider, Continental Landscapes and, following feedback, the Council has been discussing with the company how to keep residents better informed.

— read more

Annual voter registration commences


All households in Richmond upon Thames have been sent a form asking them to check that the information held on the electoral register about those living at that address is correct. This is part of the Council's annual voter registration canvass and aims to make sure the electoral register is as up-to-date and accurate as possible.
