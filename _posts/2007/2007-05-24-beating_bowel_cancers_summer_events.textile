"Beating Bowel Cancer":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/charity/200702020407 is a national charity based in St Margarets and are currently targeting the London Borough of Richmond to raise awareness of the symptoms of bowel cancer, it is the second biggest cancer killer yet over half the population are unaware of it but if caught early has a 90% success rate.

Here is a list of events at which they are having a presence or if any group that meet where the age is 55 and over they will come out and give a free talk and dvd showing.
 
* 28th May Twickenham Green Fair
* 14th June Sheen Medical Centre
* 17th June Strawberry Hill Fair
* 14th July St Margarets Fair
