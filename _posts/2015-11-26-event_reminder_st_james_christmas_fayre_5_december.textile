The St James Christmas Fayre is on "Saturday, 5<sup>th</sup> December from 12pm till 4pm":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/fair/200705145280. This year we are having an outdoor Christmas Market with lots of exciting stall holders selling art, craft, handmade Christmas decorations, prints, canvasses, cards, handmade jewellery and accessories and much more!

Our school Fayre is going to be lots of fun for the children with the Games zone, Art and Craft zone, balloon modelling, 'Snowmen' cinema and Santa's Grotto, children's present Wrapping Room to name a few. The adults and children lunches will also be available together with Irish coffee, mulled wine and hot chocolate for the children!

h4. More Information

* Entrance &pound;2 for an adult, children go free
* Tickets to be sold on the door
* 260 Stanley Road, Twickenham
