According to the ongoing _The Active People Survey's_ interim results, Richmond Bourough has 31.6% of the populations exercising for 30 minutes, three times a week.  This is compared to 27.3% in Wandsworth and 24.5% in Kensington & Chelsea.  Greenwich was last with only 11.9%. 

More detailed results will be available in December 2006.  The survey is sponsored by "Sport England":http://www.sportengland.org/.
