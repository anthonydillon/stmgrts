Orleans House Gallery is running a couple of 3 week courses over April.

There are workshops specifically for people with learning difficulties and afternoon sessions open to all.  The courses are &pound;30 each for 3 sessions and each session runs for 2 hours.  There are two sets of classes:

* Mixed Media
** Clay
** Casting
** Painting
* Printmaking
