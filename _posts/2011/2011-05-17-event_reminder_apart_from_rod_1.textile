h4. Hurry and get your tickets now!

_A Musical Celebration of the Original Rod Stewart Group_

The David Adams Appeal Fund are organising a live music gig on "20 May 2011 at York House":http://stmargarets.london/event/show/200705142766 by a fantastic new band "Apart from Rod":http://www.apartfromrod.com. The band is made up of original musicians from Rod Stewart's band.  They're playing at Clarendon Hall, York House prior to heading off for an international tour. All proceeds go to The Royal Marsden Hospital.
