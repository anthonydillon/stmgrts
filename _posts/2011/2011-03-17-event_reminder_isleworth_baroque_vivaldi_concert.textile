"A Concert of Music by Vivaldi at St Margarets Roman Catholic Church on Thursday 31<sup>st</sup> March at 7.30pm.":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/concert/200705142732

Our programme will include some well-know choral pieces (Gloria, Magnificat, Beatus Vir), some beautiful instrumental pieces (Concerto Grosso, Concerto for two trumpets), some delightful solo arias and - new for 2011 -  our 'Young Isleworth Baroque' group of younger voices.

Tickets at &pound;10 are on sale online, visit  "www.isleworthbaroque.co.uk,":http://www.isleworthbaroque.co.uk, or to personal callers only at "yellowwedge cheese":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/grocery/200711270904. For more details call 020 8943 5261.
