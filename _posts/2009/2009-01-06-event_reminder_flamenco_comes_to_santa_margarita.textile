"Flamenco classes":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/music/200901061611 are coming to St Margarets, and to get you in the Spanish spirit we would like to invite you to our "Fiesta on Wednesday 14<sup>th</sup> January, 7.30pm - 9.00pm":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/exhibition/200705142022, at The Winchester Hall, Winchester Rd.

Admission free!
