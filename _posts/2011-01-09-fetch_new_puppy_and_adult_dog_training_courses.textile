"Fetch!":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/other/200612121618, St Margarets very own dog training school, is starting new courses on Tuesday 18<sup>th</sup> January at St Margarets Catholic Church Hall.

The puppy course includes an introduction to basic training, socialisation and canine communication, whilst our course for older dogs can help you improve your training techniques and build a better relationship with your dog.

These long-established six week courses are run by Beverely Saucell BSc (Hons) a highly qualified trainer and pet behaviour counsellor.  Spaces are limited, so please book in advance.  For more information call Beverley on 020 8892 2435, email "beverley@fetchpets.co.uk":mailto:beverley@fetchpets.co.uk or visit "www.fetchpets.co.uk":http://www.fetchpets.co.uk.
