On Friday, 19 January, the St Margarets and North Twickenham Police Liason Group are holding a "Community Party":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/Party/200701070628 in Winchester Hall at the Turks Head Pub.  They have booked a DJ who will be playing loads of fun music from the 70's, 80's and 90's a there will be a cash bar all night.

Everyone is invited, you just have to get a ticket by contacting the Safer Neighbourhood Team at 020 8721 2912 or 07879 432330 or emailing  "stmargarets.northtwickenham.snt@met.police.uk":mailto:stmargarets.northtwickenham.snt@met.police.uk.   You must be over 18.
