Come to an old fashioned _barn dance_ from "7.30 to 11.30pm on 18<sup>th</sup> June 2011":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/event/200705142797 with caller and music by "Fire in the Mountains" in The Parish Hall, St Margarets Church.

Bring a picnic for the garden if the weather is fine.  Also, a bar will be available in the Hall.

<em>Proceeds in aid of the Church Building Fund and "Music in Prisons":http://www.musicinprisons.org.uk</em>
