Sunday Afternoon Art family activities are running at "Orleans House Gallery":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/gallery/200506150208. They are on the first Sunday of every month from 2pm to3.30pm.  No booking is necessary, you can just drop in any time.



h4. More Information

* &pound;1.50 per extra child
* In the Coach House (opposite the caf&#233;).
