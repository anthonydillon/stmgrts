h3. What to play at the Christmas Fair?

The 2010 St Margarets Christmas Fair takes place on "Sunday 5<sup>th</sup> December in Crown Road":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/fair/200705142573. If you are interesting in showcasing your band or group at the fair please contact Alistair at "livemusic@blueyonder.co.uk":mailto:livemusic@blueyonder.co.uk.
