Friday, 2 June at the Queen Charlotte Hall, as part of the RACC series of Friday evening Jazz events promoted by the Jazz faculty, RACC are proud to present the "Mexican Hat Dance":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/Concert/200604230626 -- a Mexican influenced evening of Jazz featuring a host band plus guest performers.

* Come and listen and dance to a great host band with an extended timbale section and brass front line.
* Eat Mexican food and drink Mexican beer.
* Browse the special interest stalls and meet others with a common interest in Jazz and adult music education.
