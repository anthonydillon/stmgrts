It is to be held at St. Mary's School Infant's site (Amyand Park Road) on Saturday 1st December from 12 noon - 3pm.

Attractions include:

* Cake sale
* Toys and Gifts
* Tombola
* Raffle
* Bottles
* Jars of sweets
... and much, much more!

All organised by Friends of St.Mary's in aid of the school.
