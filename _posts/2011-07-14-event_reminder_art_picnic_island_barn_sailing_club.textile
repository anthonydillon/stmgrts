_New Venue - For The First Time - Island Barn Sailing Club_

On "Sunday 24<sup>th</sup> July 2011 from 11am to 4pm":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/show/200705142860 join artists Emily Burton and Sasa Marinkov at Island Barn Sailing Club for an Art Picnic.

Materials for sketching (paper, pencils, and charcoal) and drawing boards will be provided, but you are welcome to bring your own equipment if you prefer.

Don't forget to bring a picnic lunch too.


h4. Ticket prices if booked and paid for at least 48 hours before the event

* Individual	&pound;20 (Environment Trust members &pound;15)
* Family	&pound;40 (Environment Trust members &pound;30)

h4. If purchased on the day

* Individual	&pound;25 (Environment Trust members &pound;20)
* Family	&pound;50 (Environment Trust members &pound;40)

h4. Details

* Island Barn Sailing Club is located at Ray Road, West Molesey, Surrey, KT8 2LF
* Family tickets are for two or more people, at least one of whom is under 18 years old
* Buy a book of 6 tickets* and get a 20% discount
* Individual	&pound;100 (Environment Trust members &pound;75)
* Tickets can be used at any art picnic, but places need to be booked in advance
