h3. Police re-open A316 between Richmond Circus and St Margarets roundabout

bq.. The A316 has re-opened after a man who was threatening to jump off a footbridge by Old Deer Park was talked down by police.

The road was shut between Richmond Circus and St Margarets roundabout.

Police were called at 5.27am and closed the stretch of road as a precaution.
