Come along to Parvinu from Saturday 23 to Monday 25 January and "celebrate Robert Burns' 251<sup>st</sup> birthday in style":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/event/200705142357. A sumptuous menu is offered on Monday night accompanied by the traditional address to the haggis and a noggin of Johnnie Walker. The celebrations are extended over the whole weekend so you won't miss out.  For everyone who comes along in full highland wear on any evening, and that includes you ladies, will be offered a welcome drink of your choice.
