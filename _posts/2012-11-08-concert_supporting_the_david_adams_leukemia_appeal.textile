"The David Adams Leukaemia Appeal Fund":http://www.davidadamsleukemiaappeal.org is a small charity based in St Margarets, Twickenham, which raises &pound;150,000 - &pound;200,000 every year for The Royal Marsden Cancer Hospital.

St Margarets based artists, Lilly Dufter-Wimmer, Moir Leslie, Ester Kosar and Roy Stratford would love to enchant you for "an evening with classical music and poetry":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/concert/200705143681 on 1 December 2012.

Come and enjoy with us pieces by Handel, Bach, Mozart, Gounod, Enesco, Saint-Sa&#235;ns, Caplet and more...



Also enjoy with us refreshments kindly provided by Gaia Wholefoods in the interval and after the concert.

h4. Date, time and venue:

* 1<sup>st</sup> December 2012 at 7.30pm
* Orleans House Gallery, Riverside, Twickenham TW1 3DJ
* Tickets &pound;20 - pay at the door on the night
* Reserve with Ester on "ester.kosar@gmail.com":mailto:ester.kosar@gmail.com or  078 2577 3179

_All the profit goes to The David Adams Leukemia Appeal Fund_
