After 19 years, "due to excessive work load" the Herdman Family Carpet Shop is moving from No. 24 Crown Road to a larger shop at No. 35 Crown Road (formerly Chrysalis Gallary &amp; Framing).  The opening day will be on the 10<sup>th</sup> of October. Still run as a family business.
