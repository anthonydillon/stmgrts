Come and meet a Palestinian Gandhi "at the Richmond Unitarian Church at 7.30pm on 13 December 2012":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/event/200705143699.  Mousa abu Maria, one of the leaders of the growing Palestinian grassroots, non-violent activism campaign of resistance to the Israeli military occupation. He is from Beit Ummar in the West Bank where an Israeli settlement has taken 65% of his community's land. He is married to a Jewish Israeli woman and works closely with many Jewish Israeli peace activists.
