Remember, this Sunday 14<sup>th</sup> June from 11.00am - 5.00pm, the St Margarets traders association is hosting a "family fun day":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/fair/200705142122.

The event will takes place in Crown Road and offers a day of family fun and entertainment including live music, competitions, fun fair, side stalls, games and a talent show.

The event has been arranged to promote "Lynn's Bowel Cancer Campaign":http://stmgrts.org.uk/directory/charity/200702020407.
