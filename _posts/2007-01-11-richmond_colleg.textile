Richmond College Community Choir is up and running again after its successful debut in the RUTC College "Music at Christmas" concert. Practices take place at Richmond College Music Centre, Egerton Road. Wednesdays, 6.30 - 8.00pm; &pound;2.00 a session, &pound;1.00 concessions. All very welcome, and no experience necessary. This term's programme includes Faur&eacute;, Cole Porter, spirituals and more besides.
