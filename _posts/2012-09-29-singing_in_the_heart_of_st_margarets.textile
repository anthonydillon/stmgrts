"Richmond Community Choir":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/music/201205311730 has a new home. Open to all those who'd like to sing, the Choir is now meeting at "Orleans Primary School":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/school/200506280125 (formerly Orleans Infant School), Hartington Road, from 6.30pm to 8pm on Thursdays.

We're currently learning a range of pieces for our Christmas concert on 13 December, ranging from the 16<sup>th</sup> to 20<sup>th</sup> centuries. More information available from "richmondchoral@gmail.com":mailto:richmondchoral@gmail.com or call 01737 243791. You would be very welcome!
