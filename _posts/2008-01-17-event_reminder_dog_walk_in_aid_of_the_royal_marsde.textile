Keen dog walkers from Marble Hill Park are joining together to walk in aid of The Royal Marsden Hospital after the recent devastating fire.
 
The walk will take place on January 27th in Richmond Park and those interested to take part can email "adams.appeal@virgin.net":mailto:adams.appeal@virgin.net to ask for a sponsorship form or to make a donation to the address below.
 
More info can be found on "www.justgiving.com/marblehill_dogwalkers":http://www.justgiving.com/marblehill_dogwalkers
