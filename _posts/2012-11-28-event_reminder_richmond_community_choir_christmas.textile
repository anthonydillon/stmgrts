Local singing group "Richmond Community Choir":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/music/201205311730 is joining forces with the Martineau Brass Quintet for a delightful concert of Christmas related music on "13 December 2012, at the church of St John the Divine (near Richmond Station) from 7.30pm":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/concert/200705143700.  Tickets are &pound;5 on the door.

The Choir is now based at Orleans Primary School in St Margarets, and anyone who would like to sing, irrespective of experience, is very welcome to participate.  The Choir has a termly changing repertoire, ranging over six centuries.   For further information contact our Director, Chris Mitchell, via email at "richmondchoral@gmail.com":mailto:richmondchoral@gmail.com, or on 01737 243791.
