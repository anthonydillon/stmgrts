Richmond Community Choir, under the direction of Chris Mitchell, is performing with local vocalist Hattie Whitehead and guitarist Tom Varrall at a concert on "Thursday 16 May 2013 at Orleans Primary School Hall, Hartington Road, St Margarets":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/show/200705143886.  The programme, which starts at 7.30pm, and is entitled "Summer Serenade" includes show songs, classical pieces and compositions by Hattie and Tom.



h4. More information

* Entry is free, with a donation box
* there will be a bar. 
* starts at 7.30pm 
* at Orleans Primary School.
