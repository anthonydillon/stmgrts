Help us build a history of Marble Hill as a public space

Have you got old memories, photographs, and documents of how you and your family have used Marble Hill Park? Come along and share them with us. We're here for you to drop them on Thursday 13<sup>th</sup> July 4- 7pm and Saturday 15<sup>th</sup> July 10-1pm.

We'll take pictures and scans and you can tell us your story!
