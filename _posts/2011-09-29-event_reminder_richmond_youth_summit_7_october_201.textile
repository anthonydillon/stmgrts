As a precursor to _Local Democracy Week 2011_ Richmond Youth Partnership is hosting, on 7 October 2011, a "Richmond Youth Summit":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/meeting/200705143064 with Dr Vincent Cable MP, Zac Goldsmith MP, Susanne Rauprich (NCVYS), Richmond Youth Forum and young people from across Richmond. To feature key note speeches, Q and A, themed workshops and discussion on the future of youth services and support in Richmond.

If you are interested in attending, you need to sign-up quickly.
