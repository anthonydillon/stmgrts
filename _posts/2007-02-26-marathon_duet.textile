Rachel Evans, a local woman, is running in the London Marathon in April.  She has committed to raise &pound;2000 for the "NCH":http://www.nch.org.uk/, 'the children's charity' and "St Martin-in-the-fields Building Project":http://www2.stmartin-in-the-fields.org/page/building/support.html.  As Rachel happens to be a soprano with St Martin-in-the-fields, to help raise funds  she has organised "A Concert for Mother's Day":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/event/Concert/200702261611 -- an evening of Arias Duets and Trios from Oratorio, Opera and Shows at "St Mary the Virgin Church":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/church/200505180329 in Twickenham on 18<sup>th</sup> March.  Rachel will be performing with Loretta Hopkins with special guest Gregory Tassell.

So if you can, please go to the concert at 7.30pm, Sunday 18th March (suggested donation &pound;8) or if you can not, donate "online":http://www.justgiving.com/speedy_soprano_rachel.
