1<sup>st</sup> St Margarets Scout Group are holding a "Good as New sale":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/sale/200705142234 to raise funds for the Pack.  We will be selling good quality clothes (adult and children), books, toys and bric-a-brac.

*Please come along and grab a bargain.*

If you have items to donate these can be brought to the "St Stephens C.E. Junior School":http://www.stmgrts.org.uk/directory/school/200507170243 hall on the morning of the event.  If you are unable to do this please call Jo Kitson (8891 2110) or email her on "johanna_kitson@hotmail.com":mailto:johanna_kitson@hotmail.com and she will arrange for bags to be picked up.
