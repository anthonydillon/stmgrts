We are looking for dancers, singers, musicians and anything else that would be suitable for our carnival.

"Carnival is on Sunday 13<sup>th</sup> June":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/party/200705142445 and takes place in the grounds of Orleans House Gallery.

Please email "Anna.Ramsay@richmond.gov.uk":mailto:Anna.Ramsay@richmond.gov.uk to register your interest.
