To celebrate the amazing new Wendy House and play equipment, the Turk's  Head is holding a "Children's Picnic":http://stmgrts.org.uk/event/concert/200705141715 on 21<sup>st</sup> October

Mark and his guitar will be holding a sing-a-long in the Garden from 12.30.  Diddy Dance and Music Bugs groups will be held from 1pm.

If you RSVP, your kids can have a free lunch.  To RSVP, email "turkshead@fullers.co.uk":mailto:turkshead@fullers.co.uk and let them know you are coming and how many young ones you will have with you.
