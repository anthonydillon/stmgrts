bq. Ticket office hours at South West Trains (SWT) stations in London and Surrey will be cut by 219 hours a week, new figures show... Ticket offices at Barnes, Hampton and St Margarets will close up to an hour and a half earlier at night. None of them will be open after 7pm. 

|Current Weekly Hours|New Weekly Hours|Difference|
|82 Hours 40 Mins|76 Hours 10 Mins|6 Hours 30 Mins|
