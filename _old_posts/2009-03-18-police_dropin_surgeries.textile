Your local St Margarets & North Twickenham Safer Neighbourhood Team is holding Drop-in Surgeries every Thursday at the following locations:

* *St Margarets Train Station*
The 1st Thursday of every month
6.00 pm- 7.00 pm
* *L'Amandine Caf&eacute;*
Crown Road
The 2nd Thursday of every month
10.00 am- 11.00 am
* *V. Thomas Butcher*
St Margaret's Road
North Twickenham
The 3rd Thursday of every month
11.00 am- 12.00 noon
* *Eurowash Launderette*
Whitton Road
The last Thursday of every month
2.00 pm- 3.00 pm

Come along to discuss any local concerns or issues and to receive crime prevention information.

You could have your mobile phone registered and your bike marked for FREE.
